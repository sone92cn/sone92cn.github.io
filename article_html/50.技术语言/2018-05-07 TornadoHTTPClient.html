Tornado HTTPClient

1、阻塞型客户端

from tornado.httpclient import HTTPClient
client = HTTPClient()
response = client.fetch("http://127.0.0.1:80/login",method="GET")
response = client.fetch("http://127.0.0.1:80/login?t=t",method="POST", body="user=a&pswd=w")
print(response.body)
